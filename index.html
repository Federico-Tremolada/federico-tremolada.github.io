Project

Enthesis FEM Model

Finite Element model of the tendon-to-bone interface (enthesis), investigating stress distribution and elastic energy in graded materials.

Tools: Abaqus, Python, MATLAB

Results show that functionally graded materials significantly reduce stress concentrations compared to sharp interfaces.
